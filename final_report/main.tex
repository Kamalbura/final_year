% --- ABSTRACT ---
\newpage
\begin{center}
    \textbf{Abstract}
\end{center}
\vspace{0.3in}
Air quality forecasting is a critical challenge in modern urban environments, impacting public health and policy-making. This project presents a state-of-the-art (SOTA) platform for multivariate air quality prediction, transitioning from classical statistical baselines to advanced deep learning architectures including Transformers, Mamba, and Spatio-Temporal Graph Networks. The system incorporates rigorous 2025 data standards, using SAITS for imputation and physics-informed loss constraints. Furthermore, we demonstrate the feasibility of Edge AI by deploying quantized models on a Raspberry Pi, achieving high-accuracy real-time forecasting with minimal latency.

\vspace{0.2in}
\noindent \textbf{Index Terms}--- Air Quality Forecasting, SOTA, Edge AI, Raspberry Pi, Deep Learning, Transformers, Mamba, Quantization, ONNX.
